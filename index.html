Camellias

Camellia are flowers with glossy leaves and roselike flowers of the tea family rnamental greenhouse shrub. Camellia can be found growing in Alabama along with other southern states in the United States.

Marigolds

Marigolds are flowers with showy flower heads with long-lasting ray flowers of the tropical American composite herbs and low shrubs. Marigolds can be found growing in Louisiana among other parts of the United States.

Zinnias

Zinnias are flowers with showy usually yellow, orange, or maroon flower heads of a genus of composite herbs. Zinnias can be found growing in North Carolina along with other southern states in the United States.
